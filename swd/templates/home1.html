About

The Student Welfare Division at BITS Pilani, K.K. Birla Goa Campus shall always strive to provide the most conducive environment to bring out the best out of every student. It is our endeavor to do anything and everything possible to make the student's stay in the campus the most memorable one.

Our division cares for every student and shall always look into their needs that may arise from time to time.


Other activities of the Division include:
  • • Collection of fees
  • • Disbursal of Merit Scholarships and MCN Scholarships
  • • Coordinating for various Fests organized by students
  • • Maintenance of Individual students account related to Mess Advance
  • • Hostel accommodation to students and leave related issues
  • • Issue of Receipts, ID Cards, bonafide Certificates & No Objection Cetificated
  • • Monitoring the Student Activity Center
  • • Processing of Medical Insurance claims
